About Mentro

Mentro is more than just a mood tracker. It’s your safe space to understand, express, and heal your emotions through calming tasks, visual reflections, and personalized guidance — one ripple at a time.

✨ What You Can Do With Mentro

  • 🌈 Track Your Emotions with Ripples
  • 📊 See Your Mood Analytics
  • 🧘 Get Personalized Healing Suggestions
  • 🎯 Complete Tasks and Celebrate Progress
  • 📅 Timeline & History
  • 🔐 Biometric Protection for Private Entries
  • 🖌️ Creative Emotional Expression
  • 📷 Gratitude Snaps & Kindness Bombs
  • 🏆 Your Emotional Achievements Matter

💡 Why Mentro?

Because emotional health isn’t one-size-fits-all. Mentro was built with care, inspired by real struggles and personal healing. Whether you're going through anxiety, heartbreak, stress, or simply want to understand yourself better — this app walks with you.

You don’t have to figure it all out today. Just ripple, reflect, and heal — one moment at a time.
